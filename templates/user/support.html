Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right herbal remedy for my condition?

We recommend scheduling a consultation with one of our certified herbal practitioners. They can assess your specific needs and recommend personalized herbal solutions. You can also browse our products by health concern categories on the products page.

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping (2-day) is available for an additional fee. International shipping varies by location but typically takes 7-14 business days. You can track your order from your dashboard.

Are your herbal products safe to use with prescription medications?

While our herbal remedies are natural, some may interact with prescription medications. We strongly recommend consulting with your healthcare provider and our herbal practitioners before starting any new herbal regimen. Always disclose all medications you're taking during consultations.

Can I reschedule or cancel my consultation appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 24 hours before your scheduled time without any charges. You can manage your appointments from your dashboard or contact our support team for assistance.

What is your return policy for herbal products?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products in their original packaging. For opened products, we accept returns within 14 days if you're unsatisfied. Herbal consultations are non-refundable but can be rescheduled. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
