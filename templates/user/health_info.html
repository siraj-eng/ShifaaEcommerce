Usage Guidelines

Proper usage is essential for safety and effectiveness:

  • Always follow recommended dosages
  • Consult with a practitioner for personalized advice
  • Be aware of potential interactions with medications
  • Store herbs properly to maintain potency
  • Start with small doses when trying new herbs

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products safe?

All our products are sourced from reputable suppliers and tested for quality. However, always consult with a healthcare practitioner before use, especially if you have existing health conditions.

How should I store herbal products?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep containers tightly sealed to maintain freshness and potency.

Can I use herbs with prescription medications?

Some herbs may interact with medications. Always inform your healthcare provider about any herbal supplements you're taking.
